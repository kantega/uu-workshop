Progress bars don't have their own semantic tag, but they have a role that can be used. If you give the div a role="progressbar", it will be readable by the screen reader. Here, you can also use ARIA attributes to provide more information. aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="3" aria-valuenow="1" will make the screen reader read something like
"33 percent progress bar"
. Additionally, if you add aria-labelledby="progress-label", it will read
"Progress - 33 percent progress bar"
.
